Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect only the information necessary to provide the service:

  • Account information: your name and email address when you register.
  • Expense data: amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data: basic logs such as login timestamps and page visits, used solely for security and debugging.

We do not collect payment information, location data, or any data from your device beyond what is described above.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to operate and improve Spendly:

  • To display your expense history, summaries, and reports within the app.
  • To authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • To identify and fix bugs or performance issues.

We will never use your data for advertising, profiling, or sell it to any third party.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers. We apply industry-standard practices including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and access controls to protect your information from unauthorized access.

Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The only external resource loaded is Google Fonts (for typography), which may log your IP address per Google's own privacy policy. No financial or personal data is shared with Google or any other party.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, want to request access to your data, or would like your account deleted, please reach out to us. We are committed to responding promptly and transparently.

You can contact us via the support link in your account dashboard or by emailing us directly at the address listed there.
